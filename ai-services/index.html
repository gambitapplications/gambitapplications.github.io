Questions

The things people ask first.

Will the AI send things without me knowing? +
No — that's the whole design. The AI drafts and prepares; nothing goes out until you approve it. Every action it takes is logged, so you can always see exactly what happened and when.
Do I have to switch away from my current tools? +
No. Gambit systems connect to what you already use — your email, calendar, CRM, forms, and spreadsheets. We build the connections between them rather than replacing them.
I'm not technical. How much do I have to manage? +
Almost nothing. Your job is approving drafts from your phone and telling us what's annoying you. We run the system, watch it, and improve it weekly — that's the managed part.
How fast can something useful be live? +
The first working piece — usually intake plus AI-drafted replies — typically lands within two to three weeks of the audit. We ship the smallest thing that saves you real time, then build from there.
What does it cost? +
It depends on the size of the system, which is exactly what the audit figures out. The audit itself is short, free, and ends with a concrete picture of what we'd build and what it would cost.
